NYALI BEACH

Nyali Beach is a residential area located in Mombasa County, connected to Mombasa Island by the New Nyali Bridge. Nyali is known for the high-class hotels and beautiful residential houses and long white sand beaches.

SEREGETI NATIONAL PARK

The Serengeti National Park is a large national park in northern Tanzania that stretches over 14,763 km (5,700 sq mi). It is located in eastern Mara Region and northeastern Simiyu Region and contains over 1,500,000 hectares (3,700,000 acres) of virgin savanna. The park was established in 1940.
